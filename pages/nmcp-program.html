The Path to Mathematical Excellence

The National Math Competition Preparation (NMCP) program represents the pinnacle of mathematical training at RSM. This invitation-only program is designed for students who have demonstrated exceptional ability and are ready to compete at the highest levels of American mathematics competitions.

NMCP students receive intensive, Olympiad-level training from our most senior coaches. The curriculum focuses on mastering the complex proof-based problems found in elite competitions, with the primary goal of qualifying for the AIME and ultimately competing in the USA(J)MO.

Competition Progression

The journey to mathematical olympiad excellence

AMC 10/12

Master advanced problem-solving techniques for the American Mathematics Competitions, the gateway to elite competitions.

AIME

Develop skills for the American Invitational Mathematics Examination, requiring deep mathematical insight and creativity.

USA(J)MO

Reach the pinnacle with training for the USA Mathematical Olympiad, featuring the most challenging proof-based problems.

Advanced Curriculum

Core Topics

Advanced Number Theory

Modular arithmetic, divisibility, prime factorization, and Diophantine equations

Combinatorics & Graph Theory

Counting principles, generating functions, combinatorial optimization

Abstract Algebra

Group theory, ring theory, field extensions, and polynomial algebra

Advanced Geometry

Projective geometry, trigonometric identities, and complex geometry

Individual guidance from experienced olympiad coaches

Admission Requirements

NMCP is highly selective and admission is by invitation only.

Prerequisites

  • AMC 10/12 score above 120
  • AIME qualification preferred
  • Strong performance in MCP program
  • Demonstrated passion for mathematics

Selection Process

  • Advanced placement examination
  • Interview with head coach
  • Review of competition history
  • Commitment to intensive training
