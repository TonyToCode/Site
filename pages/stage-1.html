STAGE 1

Foundational Spark

Grades 3-6

Focus on confidence and a love for creative problem-solving via competitions like Purple Comet & MOEMS.

Stage Overview

Learning Objectives

  • Build confidence in mathematical problem-solving
  • Develop logical reasoning and critical thinking skills
  • Foster a love for creative and non-standard problems
  • Prepare for Purple Comet and MOEMS competitions

Key Competitions

  • Purple Comet Math Meet: Team-based online competition
  • MOEMS: Mathematical Olympiads for Elementary and Middle Schools
  • Local Math Contests: Building competition experience

Sample Problem

Use image 1.

Problem:

In the morning, 5 travelers in RSMland each had the same fully charged GPS device. By sunset, Zeno had used his GPS as much as Mira and Luma combined. Zeno's battery ran out completely. Theo didn't use his GPS at all. Which device belonged to Orion?

Teaching Approach:

Start with the clue: Theo didn't use his GPS → he gets device 1.

Zeno ran out of power → he used the most → gets device 5.

Zeno = Mira + Luma → so Mira and Luma used 2 and 3 (since 2 + 3 = 5).

That leaves device 4 for Orion.

Confirm all clues match.

Mathematical Confidence
