Advanced Functional Equation

Find all functions f: ‚Ñù ‚Üí ‚Ñù such that for all real numbers x and y:

f(x + y) + f(x - y) = 2f(x)f(y)

Solution Approach:

Step 1: Analyze special cases (x = y = 0, y = 0, x = 0) to find constraints on f.

Step 2: Use substitution techniques to derive additional functional relationships.

Step 3: Apply continuity or other regularity conditions to narrow down solutions.

Result: The complete solution set includes f(x) = 0, f(x) = 1, f(x) = cos(ax), and f(x) = cosh(ax) for real constants a.
